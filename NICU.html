Goal: Maintain Glucose > 45 mg/dL (Pre-feed). Screening @ 1-2 hours of life.

🚨 IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED

  • 2️⃣ Start Continuous IV Infusion (GIR 6–8)
  • 3️⃣ Recheck glucose in 30 minutes

🫁 NRP Quick-Reference (The Golden Minute)

0–30 Seconds
INITIAL STEPS

  • Warm, dry, stimulate.
  • Position airway, suction (if needed).
  • Check HR (Listen 6s, multiply x10).

30–60 Seconds
THE ACTION

  • HR < 100 or Apneic ⮕ PPV
  • Room Air (21%) for ≥ 35w
  • 21–30% O2 for < 35w
  • MR. SOPA if chest not rising.

> 60 Seconds
THE CRITICALS

  • HR < 60 ⮕ Compressions (3:1)
  • Increase O2 to 100%.
  • Intubate / Laryngeal Mask.
  • Epinephrine if HR still < 60.

📝 TPN Order Template (EPIC)

Follow this exact sequence for Week 2-3 order entry:

  1. 1. Fluid: Input TFG (ml/kg). Epic calculates Total.
  2. 2. Dextrose: Start GIR 4-6 (Peripheral max 12.5%).
  3. 3. Protein: Start Amino Acids @ 3-4 g/kg.
  4. 4. Lipids: Start @ 1-2 g/kg (5-10 ml/kg).
  5. 5. Electrolytes: Na(2-4), K(2-3), Ca(200-500), Mg(0.3-0.5). *Wait for DOL 2-3 for Na/K*.
  6. 6. Additives: Heparin (if PICC 0.5 u/ml), Zinc, Levocarnitine (if BW < 1kg).

🚨 The "Bridge" (Starter TPN)

For < 2000g or < 32 weeks: D5 or D10 + 4g AA + Ca Gluconate + Zinc. Available in ~1hr.

🍼 Enteral Feeding Rules

  • Donor Milk: Use if <30w and no EBM. Stop @ 33w or 1500g.
  • Trophic Feeds: 10–20 ml/kg/day. Start preterms after 48h if stable.
  • NPO Triggers: Hold during transfusions, PDA treatment (Neoprofen), or poor perfusion (HIE/Dopamine).
  • Concentration: 22 kcal @ 80-100 ml/kg; 24 kcal @ 120 ml/kg.

☢️ Screening & Medical PDA

  • HUS Schedule: <32w: Days 3, 7, 14, 28.
    >32w: Days 3, 7, 28.
  • Neoprofen (PDA): 10/5/5 mg/kg over 3 days. Must monitor UOP/Cr and keep NPO.

🛡️ Strict Rules of the Unit

Follow these strictly to maintain patient safety and unit harmony.

  • ▪
    The Scrub: Mandatory 3-minute scrub (fingertips to elbows) using nail cleaners before entry.
  • ▪
    The Uniform: Remove lab coats/long sleeves or keep above elbow. No hoodies. Only Hurley-branded jackets allowed.
  • ▪
    Nails & Jewelry: NO artificial nails, shellac, or gel. Polish must be 100% intact. Remove all rings, watches, bracelets.
  • ▪
    Hygiene: No perfumes/colognes. Foam hands before/after touching any patient or bedside item. Do not enter if ill.
  • ▪
    Phones: Silent/Vibrate only. Permitted for clinical care only. Clean with wipes at morning huddle.
  • ▪
    Communication: ALWAYS talk to the bedside nurse before initiating any care or exams.
  • ▪
    Bedside: No food or drink at bedside; use staff lounge. Use bedside stethoscopes ONLY.

💉 Desired Procedures (Ask Early!)

  • Intubation: Express interest early to Attending/RT.
  • UVC/UAC Lines: Learn setup and imaging.
  • Circumcisions: Notify attending if you want to perform.
